About WEST'23

With the intention of encouraging sports and physical fitness in students and to exchange the sports culture among various colleges and finally developing sportsman spirit, we are introducing a new sports fiesta, WEST’23. 'WEST' is the abbreviation of Walchand Engineering Sports Tournament. “WEST’23” is a National level sports fest organized. It includes 15 different sports.

It includes 15 different sports viz.

• Cricket • Football • Badminton • Chess • Lawn-Tennis • Kho-Kho • Kabaddi • Basketball • Volleyball • Futsal • Box-cricket • Carrom • Hand Ball • 3A side Volleyball • Athletics • WEST Marathon.
